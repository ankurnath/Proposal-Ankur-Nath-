\chapter{\uppercase{Timeline}}

The research progress is structured into distinct phases as outlined below. Initial works
on theoretically grounded pruning~\citep{pmlr-v258-nath25a} and hierarchical
learning-based pruning~\citep{nath2025hdeeppruner} are already published. The next phase
will focus on completing the LLM2Prune~\citep{nath2025llm2prune} evaluation under knapsack
constraints and finalizing the manuscript. Final experiments and writing are expected to
conclude by fall 2026.
